Facebook urges brands to 'catch up' in mobile

In its fourth quarter, Facebook posted a better-than-expected 51.7% jump in revenues to $5.84bn, while ad revenues grew 57% to reach $5.65bn. And it is mobile that is leading Facebook's ad growth.
